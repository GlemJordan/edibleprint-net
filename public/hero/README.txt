HERO CAROUSEL IMAGES
====================
These images are displayed in the homepage hero section.
Each slide has an "after" (finished product) and a "before" (original photo).

NAMING CONVENTION
-----------------
ejemplo-{N}-after.jpg  — the finished edible print on the decorated item
ejemplo-{N}-before.jpg — the original photo the customer uploaded

SLIDE 1 — Birthday Cake
  ejemplo-1-after.jpg   : Photo of the final decorated birthday cake with edible print applied
  ejemplo-1-before.jpg  : The original flat photo/image used as the source

SLIDE 2 — Cookies
  ejemplo-2-after.jpg   : Cookies with edible print toppers (set of cookies looks great)
  ejemplo-2-before.jpg  : The original photo/logo used to print those cookie toppers

SLIDE 3 — Full Sheet
  ejemplo-3-after.jpg   : A full A4 edible sheet printed (can show it on a flat cake)
  ejemplo-3-before.jpg  : The original image printed on the full sheet

SLIDE 4 — Cupcakes
  ejemplo-4-after.jpg   : Cupcakes with small circular edible toppers
  ejemplo-4-before.jpg  : The original photo used for those cupcake toppers

RECOMMENDED SIZES
-----------------
"after" images  : Vertical/portrait orientation, ~1000 × 1150 px (matches 4:4.6 aspect ratio)
                  Min 800 px wide. Use JPG, quality 85+.
"before" images : Square, ~600 × 600 px (displayed in a 1:1 frame at ~38% of the carousel width)
                  Min 400 px wide. Use JPG, quality 80+.

TIPS
----
- "after" images fill the entire carousel frame (object-fit: cover), so the subject should be
  centred and the photo should look good when slightly cropped on the sides.
- "before" images are shown small (bottom-left corner), so they should be clear and recognisable
  at small size — a close-up or well-cropped portrait/photo works best.
- Consistent warm lighting across all "after" images will give the carousel a cohesive look.
- File size: aim for under 300 KB per image (compress with squoosh.app or similar).
